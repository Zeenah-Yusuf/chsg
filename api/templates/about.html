About Us

At CHSG Multimodal Inference, we are committed to harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to address one of the most pressing challenges of our time: ensuring safe water and healthy communities. Our platform integrates text, voice, image, and structured data analytics to deliver predictive insights that empower governments, NGOs, and local communities to act swiftly against waterborne diseases and public health risks.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI-driven public health intelligence platform in Africa and beyond, enabling proactive risk detection and fostering resilient communities where access to safe water and healthcare is a universal reality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combine cutting-edge machine learning models with real-world data sources to predict, monitor, and mitigate risks related to water quality, sanitation, and disease outbreaks. By bridging technology and social impact, we aim to provide actionable intelligence that saves lives and improves quality of living.

What We Do

  • Predictive Modeling: Using advanced RandomForest and XGBoost algorithms to forecast risks.
  • Multimodal Ingestion: Processing text, voice, and image reports from communities for real-time analysis.
  • Risk Dashboards: Delivering intuitive dashboards with charts and maps for decision-makers.
  • Alerts & Notifications: Triggering timely alerts to stakeholders when thresholds are exceeded.

Our Impact

CHSG has been designed to support sustainable development goals (SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being, SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation). By providing communities with early warnings and actionable insights, we help reduce the incidence of cholera, typhoid, and other waterborne diseases, while strengthening public health infrastructure.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Leveraging AI to solve real-world problems.
  • Integrity: Ensuring transparency and trust in our predictions.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with communities, NGOs, and governments.
  • Impact: Driving measurable improvements in health outcomes.
